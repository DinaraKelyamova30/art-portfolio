Born in Simferopol, Crimea, and currently based in Montreal, Canada, Dinara Kelyamova is a fine art artist and graphic designer.

Working primarily with oil painting, but also with mediums such as watercolor, drawing, sketches and etching, Dinara uses color, light and texture to explore themes of movement and observation in urban spaces.

With an extensive background in art, having studied at the Samokisha Art College in Simferopol, the Lviv Ivan Trush College of Decorative and Applied Arts, the Lviv National Academy of Arts, and the Academy of Art and Design in Wroclaw, Poland, Dinara’s paintings bring an academic approach to contemporary compositions and environments.

Her work has been exhibited in Lviv, Ukraine, Wrocław, Poland and in Montreal, Canada.
